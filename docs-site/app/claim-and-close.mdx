---
title: "Claiming & closing"
description: "Claim rewards and refunds, close settled accounts to reclaim rent, and sweep residual dust to the DAO treasury."
---

Once an oracle reaches `Resolved` or `InvalidDeadend`, the dApp exposes the cleanup surface: per-participant **Claim**, permissionless **Close**, and a grace-gated **Sweep**. All three wrap the action layer at `app/src/data/actions/claims.ts` and are shown on the oracle detail page (`app/src/pages/OracleDetail.tsx`).

## Claim rewards and refunds

Each participant card shows a **Claim** control, but only to the owning wallet (`authority == connected`). Claiming pays the KASS reward or refund and closes the underlying account, returning its rent. The instruction depends on the account type (`sdk/src/instructions/settlement.ts`):

| Claim | Builder | Ix | Closes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Proposer | `claimProposer` | 17 | Pull + close `Proposer`; payload `oracle_nonce u64`. |
| Fact | `claimFact` | 18 | Pull + close `Fact`; payload `oracle_nonce u64`. |
| Fact vote | `claimFactVote` | 19 | Pull + close `FactVote`; payload `oracle_nonce u64`. |

## Close accounts (reclaim rent)

Some accounts have no per-participant payout and are closed permissionlessly to reclaim rent (`sdk/src/instructions/settlement.ts`):

- **AiClaim** â€” `closeAiClaim` (Ix = 20), empty payload. The AiClaim is closed on resolution.
- **Market** â€” `closeMarket` (Ix = 21), payload `oracle_nonce u64`. Closes a **settled** Market plus its USDC escrow.

## Sweep the dust

After a grace period, the residual balances on a terminal oracle are swept to the DAO treasury and the oracle/vault accounts are closed. This is the SDK's `sweepOracle` (Ix = 22, `sdk/src/instructions/settlement.ts`):

- **Permissionless** but **grace-gated** and **governance-checked**.
- Routes the residual to the DAO treasury ATA â€” `ATA(dao_authority, kass_mint)` (`sdk/src/pda.ts` `associatedTokenAccount`) â€” and returns the oracle's rent to the **creator**.
- Payload: `oracle_nonce u64`.

<Note>
Claim is owner-gated (`authority == connected`); Close and Sweep are permissionless.
Sweep additionally requires the grace window to have elapsed and passes a governance
check. All amounts are raw base units.
</Note>
